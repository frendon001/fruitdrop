Welcome to Fruit Drop

This is a community for all people to enjoy home grown fruit and produce.

How this works

  1. Who is a fruit dropper?

    2. This is a person who grows their own fruit and would like to give it away to the community.

  2. Why would I give it away for free?

    3. We would like this to become a community where we give and exchange. So, we encourage people to exchange for other produce that you grow at home. Also, if you have a fruit tree of some sort, it's nearly impossible to eat them all. Why not share?

  3. Why would I want fruit from fruit droppers?

    4. Real, fresh, organic fruit is hard to come by. Even in our neighborhood markets nothing is as fresh and organic as the fruit being grown in our very own neighborhoods. Also, we encourage trading!

  4. Is it only fruit?

    5. No, not at all! Any home grown produce is encouraged!
